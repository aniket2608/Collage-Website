Course We Offer

We offer the best B.Tech and M.Tech courses for students by D Y Patil University

B.TECH-CE

The B.Tech in Computer Engineering is a full-time four-year (eight semesters) Bachelor's Degree program. This degree helps students build a strong academic foundation and gain practical skills necessary for advanced careers in computer engineering and technology.

B.TECH-IT

he B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) in Information Technology focuses on designing and managing advanced IT systems. This degree covers subjects such as software development and data analytics, equipping students with the skills needed to excel in the IT industry.

B.TECH AI & DS

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is a four-year undergraduate degree program. This course focuses on application of AI and data science techniques, data analytics, big data technologies, and intelligent system design.

M.TECH-CS

The M.Tech in Computer Engineering is a two-year postgraduate program designed to deepen knowledge and expertise in computer engineering. This degree emphasizes advanced topics in areas such as software engineering, preparing graduates for leadership roles and research opportunities in the field.

M.TECH-IT

The M.Tech in Information Technology is a two-year postgraduate program that focuses on advanced concepts and technologies in IT. This degree covers areas such as software development, network security and equipping students with the expertise needed for specialized roles and research in the IT industry.

M.TECH AI & DS

The M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is a two-year postgraduate program designed to provide advanced knowledge and skills in AI and data science. This degree focuses on machine learning, deep learning,preparing graduates for specialized roles in AI research, and innovative technology solutions.

Our Facilities

Facilities provided for students by D Y Patil University

Library

The library features a diverse collection of books, journals, and digital resources, along with dedicated quiet reading areas, study rooms, and computer labs.

Canteen

The objective of the Canteen and meal service is to protect by reducing the risk of foodborne illness, with proper sanitary conditions.

Workshop

The Workshop Facilities include separate areas for equipment storage, and a supervisor's quarters, along with spacious, well-equipped workstations.

Hostel

The hostel consists of a separate mess, administrative office, and warden quarters, in addition to well-furnished rooms.
